About

Currently a senior in University of Michigan majoring in Art and Deisgn and minor in UI/ UX Design.
Born in South Korea, and spending most of my youth in the United States, I have learned the values and perpectives of different cultures. I am deeply interested in photography, video editing, fashion and designing in general.
I am really sensitive with trends. I tend to work with a variety of mediums and my work is mainly informed in perceptions or contemporary issues that the world and I personally are dealing with.
